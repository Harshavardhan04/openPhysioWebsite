System Architecture Diagram

Here is our system diagram which highlights the flow of data throughout the system. It all begins with the user who interacts with our web application through their work station. Based off of how they chose to interact with our web app, the data is transmitted through 2 servers and processed further in our Base.py file to get and post requests and responses to our Json Data Base.

Sitemap Diagram

Here is our Site Map. We begin when we enter the patient ID to access the system. We then have on our dashboard five options: record a new consultation, view the latest consultation (and associated snomed meaning guidance), view details regarding all past consultations (and download a specific consultation's details), take a look at patient's progress through graphs, and update the desired SNOMED (target) values
